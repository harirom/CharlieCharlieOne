About Us

Who we are?

Charlie Charlie One is an initiative from Col.Jamie Robers who is an Ex-Veteren in Royal Navy. The main aim is to build a platform to connect the service personal and veterans who are battling cancer.  If you are a service personal and looking for a community to give and get support regarding the different types of cancer. Join our community now.Get yourself registered using the Sign up button and our admin team will notify you post verifying your details.
